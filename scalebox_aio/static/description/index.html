Scalebox All-in-One ERP

All-in-one business management for small companies (up to 10 users)

Module Overview

Scalebox ERP provides a comprehensive dashboard displaying all financial and operational indicators in one place. Designed for companies operating in Saudi Riyal needing instant visibility into business performance.

Key Features

  • Easy Sale screen: sale order + delivery + invoice + payment in one step
  • Easy Purchase screen: purchase order + receipt + bill + payment in one step
  • Sales and purchase returns with automatic credit/debit notes
  • Expenses with configurable types and printable vouchers
  • Embedded official Point of Sale with loyalty programs
  • Live KPI dashboard with financial reports (BS / P&L / Cash Flow)
  • Treasury and bank balance tracking
  • Customer receivables and vendor payables
  • Date filter with instant refresh button
  • Printable reports and PDF export
